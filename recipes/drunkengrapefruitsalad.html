Drunken Grapefruit Salad

Description

A citrus filled snack with a dash of alcohol.

Ingredients

  • 8 cups grapefruit,drained and juice reserved
  • 1/4 white sugar
  • 3 fluid ounces gin
  • 8 leaves fresh mint,minced
  • 8 whole mint leaves

Steps

  1. Mix grapefruit, 1/2 cup reserved juice, and sugar in a bowl until sugar dissolves. Transfer grapefruit mixture to 8 serving cups; top each with about 1 teaspoon gin. Sprinkle minced mint over each cup. Garnish each cup with 1 mint leaf.
  2. Enjoy!
