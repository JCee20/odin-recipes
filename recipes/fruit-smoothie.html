Delicious Fruit Smoothie

Description

A delicious mixed fruit smoothie

Ingredients

  • 1 medium banana
  • 1 1/2 cup strawberries
  • 1 cup Almond Breeze Original
  • 1/2 cup pineapple
  • 1 tablespoon almonds
  • 1 teaspoon flaxseed

Steps

  1. Puree all ingredients in a blender until smooth.
  2. Enjoy!
